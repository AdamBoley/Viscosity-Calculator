Instructions for use

  1. Select your the sample type and temperature from the drop-down menu below that most closely matches the test you are checking
  2. Click either the Ubbelohde or Zeifuchs button, depending on what viscometers you used
  3. If you are unsure what sample type to select, click here to see receive guidance
  4. Enter your run-times into the labelled boxes
  5. If you used a suspended flow viscometer, enter the constant in the labelled box
  6. If you used a pair of cross-arm viscometers, enter the constants in the labelled boxes, being sure to match them to the run-times
  7. Check your entered information and press the Calculate button
  8. The tool will calculate all of the data you need to check a viscosity test
  9. If you are using a PC, you can check the tool's functionality by right-clicking, selecting Inspect and clicking Console at the top of the window
  10. All of the inputs and outputs are logged, so you can be sure that the tool's calculations match your manual calculations
