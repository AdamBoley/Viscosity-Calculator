Viscosity Calculator

Welcome to the Viscosity Calculator, a scientific tool that automates all of the calculations in ASTM standard test method D445

Click here to open a new tab that discusses the tool and provides guidance on how to use it and get the most out of it

Use the drop-down menu below to select the calculation you want to tool to perform.

Determinability checker

Instructions for use

  1. Select your the sample type and temperature from the drop-down menu below that most closely matches the test you are checking
  2. If you are unsure what sample type to select, click here to open the guidance page
  3. Click either the
    Ubbelohde viscometer
    button or the
    Zeitfuchs viscometers
    button, depending on what viscometers you used
  4. Enter your run-times into the labelled boxes
  5. If you used a suspended flow viscometer, enter the constant in the labelled box
  6. If you used a pair of cross-arm viscometers, enter the constants in the labelled boxes, being sure to match them to the run-times
  7. Check your entered information and press the calculate button
  8. The tool will calculate all of the data you need to check a viscosity test
  9. Click the calculation details button to see the unrounded calculation values
  10. Once your have checked your data, click the reset button to clear the inputs and outputs, and reset the tool

Repeatability checker

Instructions for use

  1. Select the sample type and temperature that most closely matches the results you are checking the repeatability of from the drop-down menu
  2. If you are unsure what sample type to select, click here to open the guidance page
  3. Enter the two viscosity results into the labelled boxes
  4. Click the calculate button and the tool will perform the calculations and tell you whether the results are repeatable or not

Viscometer Calibration

Instructions for use

  1. Perform a calibration test on a viscometer using a standard calibration fluid
  2. Enter your run-times and viscometer constant in the labelled boxes
  3. Enter the viscosity of the calibration fluid that was used into the labelled box
  4. Press calculate and the tool will calculate and display the:
    • average run time
    • your observed viscosity
    • the tolerance band and associated viscosity range
    • the percentage difference between the viscosity of the calibration fluid and the observed viscosity
    • a message indicating whether or not the percentage difference is small enough that the viscometer passes its calibration check
  5. Click the reset button to clear the inputs and outputs and ready the tool to check another calibration test

Viscometer recalibration

Instructions for use

  1. Determine acceleration due to gravity at your laboratory's location
  2. Determine the acceleration due to gravity at the standardization laboratory's location
  3. Input the constant of the viscometer you wish to recalibrate
  4. Input these two values into the labelled boxes
  5. Press calculateand the tool with provide the percentage difference
  6. If the percentage difference is greater than 0.1%, the tool will then provide a new constant for the viscometer
  7. Click the reset button to clear the inputs and outputs and ready the tool to recalibrate another viscometer
