Viscosity Calculator

Instructions for use

  1. Select your the sample type, temperature and viscometer type from the drop-down menu below that most closely matches the test you are checking
  2. If you are unsure what sample type to select, click here to see receive guidance
  3. Enter your run-times into the labelled boxes
  4. If you used a suspended flow viscometer, enter the constant in the labelled box
  5. If you used a pair of cross-arm viscometers, enter the constants in the labelled box, being sure to match them to the run-times
  6. Check your entered information and press the Calculate button
